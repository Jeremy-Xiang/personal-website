how it works

◈

Theme buckets, not tickers

The portfolio is organized around investment theses — "AI infrastructure is undersupplied" — not individual stocks. NVDA, AMD, TSM, and MSFT are all expressions of the same thesis. The dashboard tracks each bucket's aggregate performance.

λ

NLP sentiment on 2,000+ headlines

Every headline from Yahoo Finance RSS, CNBC, WSJ, ProMED, CDC alerts, STAT News, and Breaking Defense is scored daily. FinBERT (fine-tuned on financial text) scores positive/neutral/negative — not VADER. Sentiment feeds directly into the prediction model.

∑

Random Forest prediction

Trained on 3 years of daily price data. Features: 5/20/60-day momentum, realized volatility, and theme-vs-SPY relative return. Predicts probability that a theme or individual ticker outperforms SPY over the next 30 days. One model per theme, one per ticker.

◉

Biodefense early warning

The Biodefense bucket specifically monitors ProMED outbreak reports, WHO news, and CDC health alerts. The thesis: mRNA platforms like MRNA and BNTX can compress vaccine development from years to months. SIGA holds the only FDA-approved smallpox antiviral. Positioning pre-event.

the thesis journal

Jan 2023 — AI Infrastructure
Initiated NVDA. AI training compute will be undersupplied for 3–5 years. GPU shortage = picks-and-shovels. Added MSFT, GOOGL, META as hyperscaler beneficiaries.
Apr 2023 — Defense
Added LMT, RTX. Ukraine conflict exposes Western stockpile depletion — multi-year re-armament cycle. Added PLTR: AI battlefield analytics is structural, not a one-time contract.
Sep 2024 — Energy Transition
Initiated nuclear basket (CEG, VST). AI data center power demand creates sustained need for carbon-free baseload. Thesis: AI → energy scarcity → nuclear.
Jan 2025 — Biodefense & Pandemic
Initiated biodefense basket. CDC tracking elevated hantavirus cases — 38% case fatality rate, no approved antiviral. mRNA platforms compress vaccine timelines from years to months. SIGA holds the only FDA-approved smallpox antiviral (TPOXX), funded by BARDA stockpile contracts.
Mar 2025 — Healthcare Infrastructure
Added TMO, DHR — lab infrastructure is picks-and-shovels for pandemic response and genomics. ABT rapid testing was proven at scale during COVID and positioned for the next outbreak.
