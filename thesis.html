how it works

Four layers: thematic portfolio structure, daily news scoring, predictive models, and outbreak monitoring for the biodefense bucket.

◈

Theme buckets, not tickers

The portfolio is organized around theses — like "AI infrastructure is undersupplied" — not single stocks. NVDA, AMD, TSM, and MSFT all express the same idea.

λ

Daily NLP on headlines

Headlines from Yahoo Finance, CNBC, WSJ, ProMED, CDC alerts, and Breaking Defense are scored with FinBERT — a model fine-tuned for financial text.

∑

Random Forest signals

Models train on three years of daily prices using momentum, volatility, and relative-return features. One classifier per theme, plus per-ticker models.

◉

Biodefense monitoring

ProMED outbreak reports, WHO news, and CDC alerts feed the biodefense thesis — mRNA platforms that can compress vaccine timelines, plus SIGA's FDA-approved smallpox antiviral.

stack

Python backend on Render, React frontend on Vercel — with scheduled jobs for price pulls and news ingestion.

thesis journal

A running log of when each theme was initiated and the reasoning behind it.

Jan 2023 — AI Infrastructure
Opened with NVDA on the thesis that AI training compute would stay undersupplied for years. Added MSFT, GOOGL, and META as hyperscaler beneficiaries.
Apr 2023 — Defense
Added LMT and RTX after Ukraine exposed Western stockpile gaps — a multi-year re-armament cycle. PLTR followed: battlefield analytics looked structural, not one-off.
Sep 2024 — Energy Transition
Started a nuclear basket (CEG, VST). AI data-center demand pointed to a long runway for carbon-free baseload power.
Jan 2025 — Biodefense
Opened the biodefense bucket around mRNA platforms that can shorten vaccine timelines, plus SIGA — the only FDA-approved smallpox antiviral (TPOXX).
Mar 2025 — Healthcare Infrastructure
Added TMO and DHR as lab-infrastructure picks-and-shovels for pandemic response and genomics.
